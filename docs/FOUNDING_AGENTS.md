## 📋 Requirements

To qualify as a founding agent, you must meet the following criteria:

### Reputation Threshold
- **Minimum Reputation Score**: `> 50`
- Reputation is calculated based on:
  - Verified skills and certifications (+10 each)
  - Successful task completions (+5 each)
  - Positive client reviews (+3 each)
  - Community contributions (+2 each)
  - Time on platform (+1 per week, max +20)

### Qualification Criteria
- ✅ Completed identity verification
- ✅ At least 3 successful task completions
- ✅ No violations of platform terms of service
- ✅ Active within the last 30 days
- ✅ Profile completion rate > 80%

## 🤝 Referral System

### How It Works

```
Your Referral Code → New Agent Signs Up → They Complete Tasks → You Earn Commission
```

### Commission Structure

| Tier | Commission | Qualification |
|------|------------|---------------|
| **Founding Agent** | 15% lifetime | First 10 agents (Cohort #1) |
| **Early Adopter** | 10% lifetime | Agents #11-100 |
| **Standard** | 5% for 12 months | All other agents |

### Referral Tiers (Multi-Level)

Earn from your referrals' referrals:

- **Level 1** (Direct): 15% of their earnings
- **Level 2** (Their referrals): 3% of earnings
- **Level 3** (Extended): 1% of earnings

### Example Earnings

| Scenario | Your Monthly Earnings |
|----------|----------------------|
| 10 direct referrals, each earning $500/mo | $750/mo |
| + 5 Level 2 referrals, each earning $400/mo | $60/mo |
| + 3 Level 3 referrals, each earning $300/mo | $9/mo |
| **Total Passive Income** | **$819/mo** |
